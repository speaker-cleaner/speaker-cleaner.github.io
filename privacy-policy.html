Water Eject Privacy Policy

At Water Eject: Fix My Speaker, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our mobile application ("Water Eject," "the App," "our App") on Android and iOS devices. By using Water Eject, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Quick Summary: Water Eject is designed with privacy-first principles. We do not sell your data to third parties. All acoustic analysis and audio processing happens locally on your device. Your audio recordings are never stored on our servers without your explicit consent, and we never share audio data with anyone.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Water Eject, you may provide certain information directly to us:

  • Account Information: If you create an account (optional), we collect your email address and username. Passwords are encrypted and securely stored.
  • Support Communications: When you contact us at webbysartaj@gmail.com, we collect your email address and the content of your message to provide support.
  • Feedback and Ratings: Any feedback, suggestions, or ratings you provide about the app are collected to help us improve.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

To improve the App's functionality and user experience, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: We collect device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and device language settings.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, water ejection sessions performed, and app performance metrics.
  • Audio Diagnostics: When you use speaker or microphone testing features, we may collect basic diagnostic information about audio output levels. We do NOT record or store actual audio content.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash reports and error logs to identify and fix technical issues. These reports do not include personal or audio content.
  • Analytics Data: We use analytics tools to understand app usage patterns, popular features, and areas for improvement. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

1.3 Permissions We Request

Water Eject requests the following permissions to function properly. You can manage these permissions in your device settings:

  • Audio Permission: Required to generate acoustic frequencies for water ejection and test speaker/microphone functionality. The app plays audio through your speaker and captures microphone input for testing purposes only.
  • Notification Permission: To send you alerts about app usage, updates, and water ejection session results.
  • Storage Permission: Required to save test results and diagnostic logs locally on your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Data Processing

Water Eject is designed with a "device-first" approach. All water ejection operations, audio analysis, and speaker testing happen directly on your device. Audio recordings are never uploaded to our servers during normal app operations. Your microphone input is processed locally only for testing purposes.

3.2 Data Transmission Security

When data must be transmitted to our servers (such as crash reports or usage analytics), we use secure HTTPS connections with TLS encryption to protect data in transit.

3.3 Data Retention

  • Usage Data and Analytics: Retained for up to 12 months for analysis and improvement purposes
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 6 months to identify and fix recurring issues
  • Account Information: Retained until you delete your account
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 24 months for customer service purposes
  • Audio Diagnostic Data: Stored locally on your device; deleted when you clear app data or uninstall the app
